About Me

I'm a PhD student in experimental AMO physics at UChicago & now in transition to Harvard. I build stuff.

Things I Build

Vacuum chamber

From 2022 to this summer (2024), I built the vacuum chamber along with MOT trap optics for a for a Rb-Cs dual-species atom array quantum simulator at the Berien lab. This is the beginning of my journey with neutral atoms.

Oh, the person in the center with a bigger smile is Jinyue who was visiting from USTC and had since taken over the experiment after my exit from Bernien Lab. The blurry dude on the right is yours truly.

Rb laser tower

I joined Giulia Semeghini's group at Harvard this fall building another dual-species neutral atom expeirment, now combining alkali Rb atoms with alkaline-earth-like Yb! Check out the rubidium MOT optics I built! Inside this 19'' rack, the saturation absorption locking and offset locking units for Rb D2-line repump & cooling are built. Cooling light is further taper-amplified to support MOT generation, imaging and optical pumping.

New adventures await!
